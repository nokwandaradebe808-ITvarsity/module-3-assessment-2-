Pink Rhubarb

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients



Steps

  1. Wash the rhubarb, trim the stalks and discard the base and any leaves. Cut the stalks into 3cm lengths. Put in alarge jar with the sugar. Shake everything around, put the lid on and leave overnight. The sugar will draw the juiceout of the rhubarb.
  2. After 24 hours, add the gin, seal and shake everything around. Leave for 4weeks before drinking. You can strain the liquor ff through a muslin-lined sieve and transfer to a bottle, but I often just leave the rhubarb and booze in the jar and ladle i into drinks that way. Over time the rhubarb and the gin go a much paler colour-this doesn't look a dramatic. The upside is you that have to get through it fairly quickly!
